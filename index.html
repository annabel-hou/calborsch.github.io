About Us

Borsch Club unites students on campus interested in exploring and connecting with the cultures of the countries in Eastern Europe and Central Asia. We are a student-run organization at Cal which aims to form a community interested in expanding their knowledge of histories, cultures, and languages by participating in cultural, social, and academic events and activities.
